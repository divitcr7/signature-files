Core tools (in order of daily reliance)

The stack below represents the workflow sequence every teammate touches: AMS360 as the source of truth, Agency Zoom to interface with clients, Microsoft Teams for internal collaboration, and Outlook for external communication.

What it is: Our primary Agency Management System (AMS) that houses every client, policy, accounting, and reporting record.

Why we use it: Acts as the system of record for service, accounting, AR, commission tracking, and management reporting.

  • Maintain client and policy records
  • Track endorsements, renewals, cancellations, and claims
  • Create tasks/suspenses for follow-up
  • Run department and management reports
  • Support accounting, AR, and commissions

What it is: Our portal for client requests plus automation engine.

Why we use it: Streamlines service, automates tasks, and improves communication between clients and teams.

  • Accept service/endorsement requests via client portal
  • Convert portal requests into AMS360 tasks and suspenses
  • Route leads by department and run campaigns (SmartCycle, Win-Back)
  • Leverage Zapier + Teams for notifications

What it is: The collaboration hub within Microsoft 365 for chat, voice, video, and notifications.

Why we use it: Keeps departments aligned, centralizes discussions, and surfaces automated alerts tied to workflows.

  • Host internal chats and daily standups
  • Spin up ad-hoc or scheduled video meetings
  • Receive workflow notifications from Agency Zoom and other systems
  • Share files and collaborate directly from OneDrive

What it is: Exchange Online accessed through Outlook for desktop and mobile.

Why we use it: Routes departmental inboxes, manages customer/vendor communication, and feeds compliance-ready records back into AMS360.

  • Manage shared departmental inboxes (Personal, Commercial, REO)
  • Track customer and vendor conversations
  • Trigger follow-up tasks or suspenses based on email activity
  • Archive important correspondence to client files

Supporting systems

Beyond the core workflow, these tools keep calls routed correctly and signatures flowing without bottlenecks.

What it is: Our cloud-based phone and routing platform.

Why we use it: Ensures calls hit the right department or representative quickly.

  • Use auto-attendant scripts for new vs. existing customers
  • Route calls by department (Personal, Commercial, REO)
  • Provide direct paths for claims, billing, vendors, lenders
  • Support round-robin or direct-to-rep call flows

What it is: Secure, compliance-ready eSignature solution.

Why we use it: Collects legally binding signatures fast with audit trails and security.

  • Send insurance applications and policy docs
  • Collect endorsements and change requests
  • Capture client authorizations and disclosures

What it is: PDF editing and document management software.

Why we use it: Prepares, edits, and organizes documents before/after signature.

  • Edit and combine PDFs
  • Prep documents for DocuSign
  • Review and store finalized files

What it is: Secure password manager for storing and sharing credentials.

Why we use it: Improves security, reduces password reuse, and allows role-based sharing without relying on chat or email.

  • Store logins for approved agency tools and vendor portals
  • Share access with team members by role/department
  • Use the password generator for strong, unique credentials
  • Maintain access continuity when roles change
